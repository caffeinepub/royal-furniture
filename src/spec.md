# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Fix product display issues and transform the Home page into a luxury furniture shopping experience with premium design elements.

**Planned changes:**
- Fix product loading and display across Home, Shop, and Product Details pages with proper error handling
- Redesign hero section with luxury typography, premium imagery, and elegant animations
- Enhance featured products section with elevated card designs, gold accents, and refined hover effects
- Upgrade categories section with sophisticated grid layout and premium imagery
- Refine testimonials section with elevated card designs and elegant styling
- Polish overall Home page with refined transitions, consistent luxury color palette, and enhanced micro-interactions

**User-visible outcome:** Users will see products loading correctly throughout the application and experience a visually stunning, luxury-focused Home page with premium design aesthetics, elegant animations, and sophisticated visual treatments that align with high-end furniture brand positioning.
